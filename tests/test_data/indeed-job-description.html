Junior Full Stack Engineer

Level2

Full Job Description

Job Description:

At Level2, our mission is remission for people with type 2 diabetes. Level2 is an all-new, personalized digital care experience complete with coaching and clinical care, and informed by the latest in diabetes technology/wearables. With real-time information on how food, movement, stress, and sleep all impact glucose levels, Level2 helps people make small changes for big impact.

Level2 is looking for a Full Stack Developer to add to our engineering team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for technology, in-depth web development and .NET core software development experiences. The individual will work in an agile environment to support short sprints and rapid delivery of releases.

Role and Responsibilities

  • Development of single page applications (SPA).
  • Integration of web pages with RESTful APIs.
  • Writing reusable, testable, quality and efficient code (and tests).
  • Participation in the full application lifecycle using Agile methodologies.
  • Development of RESTful services.
  • Active collaboration with cross-functional teams that incorporate Requirements, Design, Testing and Infrastructure Teams.
  • Proactive participation in daily development activities.
  • Estimation of detailed design and development efforts and adhering to development timelines specified in the roadmap.
  • Utilization and promotion of engineering best practices and code quality standards.

Required Education and Experience

  • Proficiency with Javascript (Current Ecmascript Standard)
  • Extensive experience with CSS and CSS preprocessors (SASS/SCSS, etc.)
  • Experience with at least one modern Javascript framework (Angular, React, Vue.js, etc.)
  • Experience consuming and developing RESTful Web Services using .NET core or similar services.
  • Proficiency and experience with Responsive Web Development
  • Understanding of UX best practices.
  • Strong adherence to coding standards.
  • Working independently and with cross-functional teams.
  • Experience with version control systems, such as Git.
  • Experience with DevOps (CI/CD)
  • Experience with Agile development methodologies, Scrum and Kanban.
  • Undergraduate or equivalent experience.

Desired Education and Experience

  • Health Care Industry & Content Management System
  • Experience with continuous delivery and integration tools.
  • Oral and written communication skills, with a demonstrated ability to communicate complex technical topics to management and non-technical audiences
  • Analytical skills, including the ability to integrate information from multiple sources.
  • Works well as a team member in a fast-paced, continually changing and often time-sensitive environment.
